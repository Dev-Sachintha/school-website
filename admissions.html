Admission Process Overview

Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward:

  1. Enquiry: Contact the admission office or visit the school for initial information.
  2. Application Form: Obtain the application form online (download below) or from the school office.
  3. Submission: Submit the completed application form along with the required documents and registration fee before the deadline.
  4. Interaction/Entrance Test: Depending on the grade level, an interaction session with the child and parents or a written entrance test may be scheduled. Details will be communicated after form submission.
  5. Admission Decision: The admission committee reviews the application and test/interaction results. Decisions are communicated via email/phone/notice board.
  6. Fee Payment: Upon selection, complete the admission fee payment within the stipulated time to secure the seat.
