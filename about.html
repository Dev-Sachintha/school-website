About Our School

Our History

Senanayake National College, located in Madampe, is a prominent educational institution in the Puttalam District of Sri Lanka's North Western Province. Classified as a 1AB school, it offers comprehensive education, including Advanced Level science streams, and currently serves over 2,700 students. ​ The college is part of the network of Central Colleges established in Sri Lanka, a concept initiated in the 1940s by C.W.W. Kannangara to provide free education across the country. These schools, known as "Madhya Vidyalaya" or Central Colleges, were designed to offer quality education in various regions, including Madampe.
